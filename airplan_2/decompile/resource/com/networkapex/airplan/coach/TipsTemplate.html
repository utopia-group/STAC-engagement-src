AirPlan Usage Tips

  • View previously uploaded route maps by clicking "Route Maps" at the top of your screen. From that page, click on a route map to see more information.
  • Calculate the number of passengers you can get from one airport to another using the Capacity calculator.
  • Delete route maps at any time by clicking "Delete Route Maps" at the top of your screen.
  • You can delete more than one route map at a time.
  • Remember to logout when you are finished!
